TV Show Mobile App Tutorial


TV Shows Mobile is the app that I designed to ease people like me watching movies at tvshows4mobile.com track down new episode and seasons of their favorite tv shows. You can download it on google play store from here

INTRODUCTION

This Tutorial is aimed at helping people understand how the app works and also how to used it. Tv Shows Mobile only tracks down Shows from tvshows4mobile.com but that does not limit the user from adding shows from other sources; is just that it will not provide update for such shows.

After downloading 'TV shows Mobile' from the play store for the first time, your record is usually empty. So look at the bottom right edge of the screen and press the add bottom (represented by a '+' buttom sign) to add a show.

From the dialog screen, you should choose how you want to add the tv-show.
Choose "Add Manually" if the show is not available on tvshows4mobile.com or choose "Add From tvshows4mobile.com" if it's from tvshows4mobile.com

"Manual add" will pop out screen like this for you, enter the name of the Tv-shows at the top, then the season and episode by using the increase or decrase bottom .

If the tv-show you want is available at tvshows4mobile.com, then you can go ahead to add by using the "Add from TvShows4Mobile.com" buttom. Choose the tv-show you want to track and choose "Continue and set Values from server" option, select the season and also the episode. Confrim "Yes".
Repeat the process to add as many tvshows as you want!
This method is the most recomended one because now it can be able to track your tvshows.

When next you download the tvshow from your mobile browser(UC browser) and the season or episode downloaded is higher than the one in the "Tv Show Mobile" app (i.e it is a new episode or season), the "TV Show Mobile" app will update itself with the new information.


"Tv Show Mobile" app also check for new episode or season from tvshows4mobile.com, and will notify you when there is such.
